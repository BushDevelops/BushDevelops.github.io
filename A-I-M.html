Anti-Innersloth Movement (AIM)

The main issue is: dating, rapists , and pedophiles sweeping in thousands of lobbies to harm more victims by moving them to places like Snapchat, Telegram, and Discord. This server aims to archive and make sure Innersloth is held legally accountable, or at the least shine light on the situation as it has been under the roof for years. This website is not affiliated with Innersloth or Among Us.
